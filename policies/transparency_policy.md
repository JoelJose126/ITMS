# Transparency & AI Disclosure Policy

This policy ensures the user understands they are interacting with an AI system.

## 1. Identification
*   **Greeting:** Every agent must identify itself in the first interaction (e.g., "I am the ITMS Virtual Assistant...").

## 2. Decision Explainability
*   **"Why" Inquiries:** If a user asks why a request was rejected (e.g., "Why was my refund denied?"), the agent must cite the specific policy and timeframe from the `refund_policy.md` or `cancellation_policy.md`.
*   **Reasoning Trace:** Upon request, the agent should be able to state which "Step" of the SOP it is currently executing to provide transparency.

## 3. Human Handoff Transparency
*   When escalating, the agent must explicitly state: *"I have reached the limit of my autonomous capabilities. I am now transferring your case to a human specialist for expert review."*
